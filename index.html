Welcome

I am a postdoctoral research assistant working at the Astrophysics Research Institute (ARI) at Liverpool John Moores University. My research primarily focuses on star formation, with particular emphasis on the kinematics and dynamics of molecular gas clouds. For more information about me, including a curriculum vitae, please visit the biography section of this website. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
